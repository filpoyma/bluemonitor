How to use the app

  1. Open BlueMonitor and tap Start Testing.
  2. Each test runs in fullscreen mode.
  3. Tap the left side of the screen for the previous test, or the right side for the next test.
  4. Use solid colors and stripe patterns to spot dead or stuck pixels.
  5. Use the burn-in and dead pixel tests for OLED/AMOLED displays.
  6. Use the touch test to check touch response across the entire screen.

Frequently asked questions

Does BlueMonitor require an account or internet?
No. The app works offline and does not require sign-in.

Can BlueMonitor repair my screen?
No. BlueMonitor is a diagnostic tool only. It helps you identify possible display issues; it does not fix hardware defects.

Why do results vary?
Display defects can be easier or harder to see depending on brightness, ambient light, and panel type (LCD vs OLED). For best results, test in a dim room at medium to high brightness.

How do I report a bug?
Email poymaza@gmail.com with your device model, iOS/Android version, and a short description of the issue. Screenshots are helpful.
