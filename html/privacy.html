BlueMonitor Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Roman Parmen ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in BlueMonitor, a mobile display diagnostic app for iOS and Android (the "App").

Summary

BlueMonitor is designed to work offline. The App does not require an account, does not show ads, and does not sell your personal information.

We do not collect, store, or share personal data through the App.

Information we do not collect

BlueMonitor does not collect:

  • Name, email address, or contact details
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers for tracking or advertising
  • Usage analytics or crash reports sent to our servers
  • Photos, files, or other content from your device

Data stored on your device

The App may keep minimal, temporary information in memory while it is running — for example, whether an on-screen hint was already shown during the current session. This information stays on your device, is not sent to us, and is cleared when you close the App.

BlueMonitor does not persist personal data to cloud storage or external servers operated by us.

Internet access

The App is intended to work without an internet connection. On Android, the App may declare the Internet permission because of the underlying mobile framework. BlueMonitor itself does not send your personal data to us or to third-party analytics services.

Third-party services

BlueMonitor does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. Apple App Store and Google Play may process information according to their own privacy policies when you download or update the App.

Children's privacy

BlueMonitor is a general-purpose utility and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Your rights

Because we do not collect personal data through the App, there is usually no personal data for us to access, correct, or delete. If you have a privacy question, contact us using the email below.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page will be revised when changes are posted here.
